# Release Checklist

- Confirm app version/build numbers are incremented for iOS/Android.
- Upload build to TestFlight / Play Console internal track.
- Verify Privacy Policy / Terms / Support URLs are public and accurate.
- Provide reviewer credentials and OTP/test instructions.
- Ensure storefront availability does not overlap between Student and Staff apps.
- Update App Store Connect metadata to clearly describe the staff role.
- Re-verify screenshots and metadata match current build.
- Respond to Guideline 4.3(a) with storefront split details.
