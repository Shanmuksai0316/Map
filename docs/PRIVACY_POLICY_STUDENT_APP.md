# Privacy Policy – MAP HMS Student App (Vidyarthi)

Last updated: March 2026  
App name: Vidyarthi (MAP HMS Student)  
Publisher: OMAP Services Management Pvt Ltd

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the MAP HMS Student application (Student App or Vidyarthi) on your mobile device. The app is intended for use by students of institutions (colleges, hostels) that use the MAP HMS platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide
- **Phone number** – Used to sign in via one-time password (OTP) and to identify you to your institution.
- **Name, student ID, hostel, room** – Provided by your institution and displayed in the app. We do not collect this directly from you; it comes from your institution's MAP HMS account.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Device and app usage** – Such as device type, operating system, and app version, to support login, security, and troubleshooting.
- **Push notification token** – So we can send you alerts via Firebase Cloud Messaging. The token is stored and linked to your account for the duration of your use of the app.

### 1.3 Information generated through use of the app
- **Activity** – Attendance, gate passes, leave requests, guest entries, complaints, and other actions. This is processed to provide the service and to maintain records as required by your institution.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:
- Authenticate you via OTP and manage your session.
- Provide the Student App services (dashboard, attendance, gate passes, notices, etc.).
- Send push notifications relevant to you.
- Operate and secure the service.
- Comply with legal and institutional requirements.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell your personal information.

## 3. Account Deletion and Data Retention

### In-app deletion initiation
You can request account deletion from within the app: **Profile** → **Request Account Deletion**. You will be asked to confirm and to enter an OTP sent to your registered phone number. Upon successful submission, your app access is revoked immediately and you are logged out.

### Deletion workflow
- **Requested** – Your deletion request is recorded.
- **Processing** – We process the request (typically within 30 days).
- **Completed** – Non-mandatory personal data is deleted or anonymised.

### What we delete or anonymise
We delete or anonymise non-mandatory personal data, including:
- Name, phone, email, and other contact details
- Student profile information
- Feedback comments and similar content

### What we retain (archived, non-active)
We retain only legally required records for security, compliance, and institutional obligations, such as:
- Audit logs (for security and compliance)
- Attendance records (anonymised where possible)
- Financial/payment records if legally required
- Incident and emergency records for safety

These retained records are held in archived, non-active form for the period required by applicable law (e.g. up to 7 years or as prescribed by your institution and local regulations).

## 4. Data Security

We use encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure storage, and OTP-based authentication. Keep your device updated and do not share your OTP.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to access, correct, request deletion, restrict processing, or lodge a complaint. To exercise these rights, contact your institution's MAP HMS administrator or us using the contact details below.
